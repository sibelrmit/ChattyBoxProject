Software Tester

Software Testers are responsible for the quality assurance of the product. They are responsible for designing scenarios and testing the software for its usability, analyzing the collected data, then reporting the effectiveness and defects of the software to the production and design team. Client interaction is also required to gain proper understanding of the requirements set by them. Software Testers need to have solid, recent knowledge of software programmes and testing designs, with effective communication skills. A bachelorâ€™s degree in Computer Science or related field is required accompanying their knowledge of testing methodologies.

The skills required for this role are the ability to test and execute plans, documenting test scenarios within the understanding of business requirements, User Interfacing, QA (quality assurance) engineering, team leading and team management, application development, knowledge and use of web services, data integrity and a thorough understanding of C++, C#, Java, SQL languages and the ability to use Jira, Scrum amongst other software development programs. Software testers will be working on an average of 40 hours full-time per week.
