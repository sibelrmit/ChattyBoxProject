Chatty Box

The ChattyBox Team have been planning a project that we are now starting to bring to life. We are a team made up of six intelligent young individuals who have specialty in business and IT, who are working together to make a state of the art AI chat bot named ChattyBox for Centrelink's services. This is our team website where you can learn all about our project with detailed explanation and reporting, and showcases of our chat bot designs and the ultimate prototype!
