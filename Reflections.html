Johnny Le

I think our team did a great job on working together to complete this project. Communication was clear and consistent throughout and we had our weekly meetings as scheduled. Everyone in our team had completed their assigned tasks without any issues. Although we did lose a team member, we were able to gain another team member as a replacement which luckily did not cause that much of a setback for us.

One of the challenges we did face was attending the meetings. We sometimes did not have all members attend the weekly meetings however we were able to fill them in on what was discussed including having the meeting recorded so that they could watch it at a later time. The workload was much heavier than the previous assignment and with our nearing deadlines for other courses, it had been increasingly difficult to complete our tasks at the agreed time. We were slightly delayed in finishing our tasks but luckily we forecasted this and had set earlier deadlines to counteract this.
