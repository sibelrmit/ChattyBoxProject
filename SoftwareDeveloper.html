Software Developer

A software developer is sometimes someone who analyses users’ needs then designs the test and develops software to fulfill those needs. You may assist with the look and development of several web based applications to collect various datasets within the applying. You should monitor the quality and performance of applications through testing and maintenance. The developer will have sufficient experience and proficiency within the online application space including experience with the relevant software tools and libraries.

The skills required for this position has an ingenious and innovative mindset meaning the flexibility to bring new software capabilities to life. You furthermore need analytical skills, this suggests having to match improvements and creations to users’ and clients’ needs. You may also need a Bachelor Degree in Computer Science/Software Engineering. Has 5+ years experience in JAVA development. Experience in CSS, HTML5, JAVAscript. Has familiarity with a range of platforms including Apache, Tomcat, MySQL, Linux. even have knowledge of code source control, especially in GIT.
