AI Specialist

An AI specialist is someone who has extensive experience and knowledge in artificial intelligence and machine learning. This role requires 6-10 years plus experience and the ability to create, work toward KPI’s, and work within a team. Additionally, it is required that you know the AI world inside and out because this role is specialised and requires a generous amount of prior experience.You will be involved in a project that brings AI to life in the form of a chat bot, meaning that you will be working on a project for a elongated period of time and in the end will be part of life changing movement and creation. We are seeking motivated individuals that are excited and keen to be part of a large project with our company.

The skills required for this position are extensive knowledge and experience in Artificial Intelligence and machine learning, hence the position title being AI specialist. Thorough communication skills and the ability to simplify technical knowledge to others, the ability to work in a team, the ability to organise and manage time and work toward KPI’s, ensure a proficient outcome when plans don’t go to plan, self-motivation and a positive attitude! You will be working full time with set hours.
